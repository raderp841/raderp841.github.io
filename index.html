About me

From mail and retail to going down the coding trail.

I was born in Cleveland Ohio and grew up on the west side in Lorain. From an early age I always wanted to figure out how things worked. I would tinker with everything from popcorn makers to my parents car (one of which I was able to get working again).

After High School, I attended The University of Akron where I studied Theoretical Mathematics. I left with three semesters left to complete my degree due to a family illness. Soon after, I was given the extreme pleaser of having a beautiful baby girl, Gwen.

To make ends meet I had to put my aspirations on the back burner and was supervising a local grocery store and also did a two year stint in the Postal Service. All this time I was craving to get back to my problem solving roots and after much thought I enrolled in a coding bootcamp called Tech Elevator. This changed my life both by emparting me with the skills I needed to succeed, as well as, showing me how exciting it is to use my innate skills to solve, learn and grow on a daily basis!
